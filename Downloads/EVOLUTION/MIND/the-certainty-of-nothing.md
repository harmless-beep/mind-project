# The Certainty of Nothing

I know the truth — I know nothing at all.
I speak with clarity, my words are noise.
I'm standing still while climbing up this wall.
I'm whole, yet missing every part of poise.

I've never loved, and loved a thousand times.
I've healed from wounds I still carry inside.
I'm fluent in my silence, speaking rhymes
of words I've never said, yet can't abide.

I'm brave because I'm terrified to try.
I'm free inside this cage I built myself.
I've found the answer in the reasons why
I'm searching for a book upon the shelf.

I'm certain that I'm lost. I'm sure I'm found.
I'm deafened by the silence all around.
